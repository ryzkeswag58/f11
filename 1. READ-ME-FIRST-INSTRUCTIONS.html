Content Locker for OGads network.

Inside your downloaded files, locate a .zip file named IG_Followers_Generator_Landing_Page_Color_Marketing_Rhino_Content_Locker_1.0.0
It is important to select a version with Content_Locker in its name as it is already included with a button with onclick="call_locker();" function, so your content locker gets loaded when user clicks the button.


Open your processing_request.php file and and place your content locker scrip, preferably inside footer, where all other JS scripts files are included.

Your human verification button, which is already included in previously mentioned version, should look like this:

<a type="submit" id="human-verification-button" class="human-verification-button ig-button" onclick="call_locker()">Verify Now</a>

Note: It is important onClick Load Method, when you are creating your OGads content locker.

The only thing left to do is to include your content locker script.

Captcha Locker for OGads network.

Inside your downloaded files, locate a .zip file named IG_Followers_Generator_Landing_Page_Color_Marketing_Rhino_Captcha_Locker_1.0.0
It is important to select a version with Captcha_Locker in its name as it is already included with a captcha locker div inside which your locker will be loaded.


Open your processing_request.php file and and place your captcha locker scrip, preferably inside footer, where all other JS scripts files are included.

Your human verification captcha locker div, which is already included, should look like this:

<div data-captcha-enable="true"></div>
